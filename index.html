A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Great Indian scientist and politician who played a leading role in the development of India's missile and nuclear weapons programs.

About the Legend

1. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam, in full Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India.

2. He served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.

3. While delivering a lecture at the Indian Institute of Management Shillong, Kalam collapsed and died from an apparent cardiac arrest on 27 July 2015, aged 83.

4. Wheeler Island, a national missile test site in Odisha, was renamed Kalam Island in September 2015.

5. A prominent road in New Delhi was renamed from Aurangzeb Road to Dr APJ Abdul Kalam Road in August 2015.
